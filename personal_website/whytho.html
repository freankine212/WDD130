But why though?

Why Do People Continue to Hunt Even Though You Can Buy Meat In Stores?

Hunting is a phenomenal way to keep the population of an animal in check. In places where hunting is not allowed, there are too many animals usually and that can cause the animal population to actually die out. When a place allows the controlled hunting of game, it can allow the population to stay in check and not overpopulate.

If there is no control, then the population will decrease until there is nothing left. By actively hunting and harvesting game, it allows for an animal population to become stronger and more fit to survive harsh elements like snow and heat as there is enough resources for them to survive during those times.
